A focused macOS tool for the surprisingly common case of "I have structured data and want to turn it into a slide deck without opening Keynote." Imports JSON, XML or plain TXT, lays them out as slides, and presents them with full-screen multi-display support and keyboard navigation.

v1.0 — production-ready with unit and UI tests.
