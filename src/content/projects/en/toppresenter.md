Professional Bible and worship presentation for macOS — a clean, native alternative to the cross-platform tools most churches end up tolerating.

Core features:

- Import from **six Bible formats** (OSIS, Zefania, MySword, USFM, JSON) and **four song formats** (OpenSong XML, OpenLyrics, PowerPoint PPTX/PPT).
- Full-text search across all loaded translations with multi-verse selection.
- Independent output window on a second display, with per-style transparent backgrounds and context-aware typography.
- 100% native SwiftUI + SwiftData — no Electron, no WebView, opens in under a second and never blocks on the main thread.

Built for the way our local church actually works on a Sunday morning, then generalised.
