A long-running side project — a faith-based platform for kids' spiritual growth, designed with the visual energy and interaction depth that children actually pay attention to.

The current public release is the v0.1 landing experience. The internal roadmap (planned through 2027) covers Bible stories, memory-verse games, a prayer journal, and a parental dashboard. Built on Next.js 16 + React 19 with Framer Motion + GSAP for the animation layer, Radix for accessible primitives, and Lenis for the smooth-scroll story.

Most of the engineering interest here is in **delivering rich interactive content while staying accessible** — animations respect `prefers-reduced-motion`, every interactive control is keyboard-navigable, and the typography is tuned for kids who are still learning to read.
