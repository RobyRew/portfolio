Higher National Certificate in Web Application Development. Builds directly on the ASIX systems-administration qualification, adding application-development depth — front-end frameworks, server-side scripting, relational and document databases, and the full deploy-monitor-iterate loop.

The combination of a systems background plus a development qualification is intentional — modern infrastructure work assumes you can read (and often write) the application code that runs on top of it.
